# SQL Analysis

This folder contains SQL queries written in Databricks SQL to analyze the cleaned Amazon sales dataset and answer key business questions.

All queries are documented and organized based on analytical themes to ensure clarity and reproducibility.

---

## 1. Sales Overview

The following questions were addressed to understand overall sales performance:

- What are the total number of orders, total sales amount, and total quantity sold?
- What is the average order value (AOV)?
- How do monthly sales revenue and order volumes trend over time?
- Which month generated the highest sales revenue?
- What is the distribution of orders by order status (Completed, Cancelled, etc.)?

---

## 2. Product Analysis

These questions focus on product-level performance:

- Which product categories generate the highest sales revenue?
- Which product categories have the highest order volume?
- Which product sizes sell the most by quantity?
- What is the average quantity per order by product category?
- What percentage of total sales comes from the top 3 product categories?

---

## 3. Fulfilment Analysis

The following questions analyze fulfilment performance and reliability:

- What percentage of orders are fulfilled by Amazon vs Merchant?
- What is the total sales revenue by fulfilment method?
- Which fulfilment method has a higher cancellation rate?
- What is the average order value (AOV) for each fulfilment type?
- What is the successful delivery rate for Amazon vs Merchant fulfilment?

---

## 4. Customer Segmentation

These questions examine differences between customer types:

- What proportion of orders are B2B vs B2C?
- How does sales revenue differ between B2B and B2C customers?
- Which customer type has a higher average order value?
- Which customer segment has a higher cancellation rate?
- What is the average quantity per order for B2B vs B2C customers?

---

## 5. Geographical Analysis

Geographic performance was analyzed using the following questions:

- Which states generate the highest sales revenue?
- Which cities place the most orders?
- What is the average order value by state?
- Which states have the highest cancellation rates?
- Which states have both high order volume and high cancellation rate?
- What percentage of total revenue is contributed by the top 5 states?

---

## 6. Time-Based & Advanced Analysis

Advanced time-based analysis includes:

- What percentage of total sales revenue does each month contribute?
- What is the month-over-month percentage growth or decline in sales revenue?

---

## Tools Used
- Databricks SQL

The results of this analysis support the insights presented in the dashboard and final report.
